Usage & Ayurvedic Benefits

Storing water in a copper vessel creates a natural purification process. It can kill all the microorganisms, molds, fungi, algae and bacteria, present in the water that could be harmful to the body. Copper is also an essential trace mineral that is vital to human health, aiding digestion and boosting the immune system.

Care Instructions

To clean your copper jug, rub the surface with a mixture of lemon juice and salt, then rinse thoroughly. Avoid using abrasive scrubbers as they can scratch the soft metal. Copper naturally oxidizes and tarnishes over time—regular specific cleaning will maintain its shine.
